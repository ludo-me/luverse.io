About Us

GraceHaven Charity Care is dedicated to transforming lives by providing shelter, education,
and emotional support to children in need.
Our goal is to empower every child to break free from the cycle of poverty and neglect, giving them a real chance at a brighter future.

Our Mission

To provide a safe haven, education, and emotional care for vulnerable children, helping them grow into confident, independent, and compassionate members of society.

Our Vision

A world where no child is abandoned, neglected, or forgotten — where communities come together to protect, nurture, and uplift the most vulnerable among us.
